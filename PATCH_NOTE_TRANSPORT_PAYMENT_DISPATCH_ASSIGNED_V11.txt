PATCH: TRANSPORT PAYMENT VERIFY + DISPATCH ASSIGNED STATUS V11

Scope:
- Built on top of rian-main-transport-self-entry-db-verify-v10-no-root.zip.
- No DB schema/RPC changes.
- No service worker/cache source changes.
- No mass update/delete for existing orders or T-codes.

Fix 1: Transport “Krye pagesën”
- Transport cash payment now routes through ARKA_ACTION.TRANSPORT_ORDER_PAYMENT and requires a verified arka_pending_payments row before UI success.
- Verification checks: payment id exists, transport_order_id matches, transport_code_str matches when available, amount matches, type=TRANSPORT, source_module=TRANSPORT, and created_by_pin matches the actor PIN.
- ARKA engine verifies the payment row after insert or duplicate detection and returns paymentVerified=true plus verifiedPayment/payment/row.
- Double tap is blocked in the transport payment screens with in-flight refs plus the deterministic ARKA idempotency key.
- If ARKA insert/update/verify fails, the UI shows a real ARKA error instead of marking payment success from local state only.

Fix 2: Dispatch-created transport orders
- Dispatch-created orders assigned to a transporter are saved as status=assigned and data.status=assigned.
- Assignment rewrites for pre-pickup transport rows now keep the row in TË REJA instead of moving it directly to PIKAP.
- Transport item/board accept/start flow moves assigned/new/inbox/pranim/dispatched orders to pickup and records accepted/started metadata.

Expected acceptance:
- T-code payment creates/returns arka_pending_payments with type/source_module TRANSPORT, status COLLECTED, transport_order_id, transport_code_str, amount, and created_by_pin.
- A dispatch-created assigned order appears in TË REJA first, then moves to PIKAP only after Tapin accepts/starts it.
