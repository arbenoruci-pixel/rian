PATCH V10 — ROUTE DEEP SCAN / SAFE LAZY LOOP FIX

Source ZIP scanned: rian-main 500 369.zip

Problem confirmed:
- /transport/pranimi was still rendered through SafeLazyRouteShell:
  safeLazyElement('/transport/pranimi', () => import('@/app/transport/pranimi/page.jsx'), ...)
- The same pattern existed on other operational ARKA/TRANSPORT routes.
- On iOS/PWA this can stall at dynamic import and show:
  MODULI NUK U NGARKUA / U NDAL GLOBAL LOOP.

Minimal fix:
- Convert daily operational ARKA/TRANSPORT routes from SafeLazyRouteShell to direct eager route elements.
- Convert thin redirect-only transport wrappers to direct <Navigate> redirects, so those wrapper modules are not imported at all.
